Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind Tamazight Woodcraft

The Tamazight Woodcraft Story

Founded in 1985 by master craftsman Hassan Amazigh, Tamazight Woodcraft began as a small workshop in the heart of Agadir, Morocco. Drawing inspiration from the rich cultural heritage of the Amazigh people, Hassan combined traditional Berber woodworking techniques with contemporary design principles.

For over three decades, we have been dedicated to creating exceptional handcrafted wooden furniture that celebrates the beauty of Amazigh artistry. Each piece we create tells a story of cultural heritage, skilled craftsmanship, and sustainable practices.

Today, Tamazight Woodcraft has grown into a renowned furniture atelier, led by the second generation of the Amazigh family. We continue to honor our roots while embracing innovation, creating timeless pieces that bring the spirit of Moroccan craftsmanship into modern homes around the world.

Our Core Values

At Tamazight Woodcraft, our values guide everything we do, from the selection of materials to the final finishing touches on each piece.

Craftsmanship

We believe in the power of human hands and traditional techniques. Each piece is meticulously crafted by our skilled artisans who have honed their craft over many years.

Sustainability

We are committed to responsible sourcing and eco-friendly practices. Our wood comes from sustainable forests, and we strive to minimize waste in our production process.

Heritage

We honor the rich cultural heritage of the Amazigh people by preserving traditional designs and motifs, ensuring these artistic traditions continue to thrive.

Innovation

While respecting tradition, we embrace innovation and contemporary design, creating furniture that is both timeless and relevant for modern living.

Passion

Our love for woodworking drives us to pursue excellence in everything we create. We pour our hearts into each piece, ensuring it brings joy to its owner for generations.

Community

We believe in supporting our local community by providing employment opportunities and training the next generation of craftspeople in traditional woodworking skills.

Our Journey

The milestones that have shaped Tamazight Woodcraft over the years.

The Beginning

1985

Hassan Amazigh opens a small woodworking shop in Agadir, focusing on traditional Amazigh furniture designs.

First Workshop Expansion

1992

Growing demand leads to our first workshop expansion and the hiring of additional craftsmen.

International Recognition

1998

Our pieces are featured in an international design exhibition in Paris, bringing global attention to Amazigh woodworking.

Second Generation

2005

Youssef Amazigh joins the family business after completing his studies in furniture design.

Sustainable Practices

2010

We implement a comprehensive sustainability program, focusing on responsibly sourced materials and eco-friendly finishes.

Online Presence

2015

Launch of our e-commerce platform, making our handcrafted furniture available to customers worldwide.
